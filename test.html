Information we use but NOT collect

For use of Parallax you may provide us and upload to Parallax your images that can contain face (photo or video). In order to make possible for you to use certain functions of Parallax, specifically finding the best focus point for 3D Photo automatically, we will process (provided by you) human face images (photo or video) through Vision Face Detection API technologies that will provide us information about human faces’ position on your image and/or video frame. Images (photo or video), that you provide us in or through Parallax, and/or other information related to human faces obtained from your images (photo or video) and/or through the use of Vision Face Detection API technologies are the “Face Data”.

We do not:

  • collect, store, share or transfer the Face Data off the User devices;
  • use the Face Data to identify any particular individual User;
  • use the Face Data for authentication, advertising, or marketing purposes, or to otherwise target a User in a similar manner;
  • use the Face Data to build a User profile, or otherwise attempt, facilitate, or encourage third parties to identify anonymous Users or reconstruct User profiles based on the Face Data;
  • transfer, share, sell, or otherwise provide the Face Data to advertising platforms, analytics providers, data brokers, information resellers or other such parties.

You hereby express your informed written consent on processing of your Face Data.
